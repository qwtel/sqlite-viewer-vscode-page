What datetime formats are supported?
PRO automatically detects and formats datetime values from the following input formats:

‚Ä¢ ISO-8601 - Standard international date format (2024-01-15T14:30:00Z)
‚Ä¢ SQLite ISO-like - SQLite's text format (2024-01-15 14:30:00)
‚Ä¢ Unix timestamps - Seconds since 1970 (1705329000)
‚Ä¢ Unix microseconds - Microseconds since 1970 (1705329000000000)
‚Ä¢ JavaScript timestamps - Milliseconds since 1970 (1705329000000)
‚Ä¢ Excel serial dates - Excel's 1900 system (45310.60417)
‚Ä¢ Julian days - Astronomical Julian day numbers (2460327.10417)
‚Ä¢ Windows FILETIME - 100-nanosecond intervals since 1601 (133312320000000000)
‚Ä¢ .NET Ticks - 100-nanosecond intervals since 0001 (638420256000000000)

Display options: Human-readable, time-ago ("6 months ago"), ISO strings, or raw values

Export integration: Dates maintain their format in exports (e.g. Excel serial dates for Excel, ISO for JSON, etc.)

Click the date icon in column headers to cycle through display formats.
Is my data uploaded to a server?
No, your data never leaves your device. All processing, including exports and copy operations, happens locally in your browser. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive information.
What are the storage limits?
Storage limits depend on your browser and device. The page automatically detects your available storage and displays it above. Most modern browsers support several GB of local storage, which is typically more than enough for common SQLite files.
